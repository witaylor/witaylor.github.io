    • PostureDetect

      This is an initial exploration into a potential idea for my final year university project. It's an app that will allow users to upload a photo of themselves and then receive a detailed analysis of their posture.
      Right now, you can upload photos and see a estimated skeleton drawn on to the image, but there is no posture analysis yet.
